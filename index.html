What I care about

Design and development of a website are more than optimizing its appearance and functionality

Your Identity

Today, a website is the most comprehensive medium to tell your audience why they should choose you as their product or service provider. While templates are an easy solution to create this medium, they usually don't reflect who you are. Implementing your identity to the design and functionality of your website is essential to have a memorable impact both on your target audience and on the web.

Accessibility

Not everyone browses the web the same way: While some people read and watch to access the content of a webpage, some use a text to speech reader to access all available content. This is just one example and there are many others concerning navigation, animations, colors, etc. I want to make sure that most people can benefit from your website as intended.

Responsiveness

Since these days almost every screen has a different ratio and resolution, websites should be able to adapt to all of them to provide a smoothly flowing user experience. Trying to read an overflowing table by scrolling between left and right frequently, or to fill a form by zooming in and out constantly is frustrating. Inattention to these details can even make a visitor leave a website and choose another provider.
